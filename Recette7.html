One-Pot Spinach & Chicken Sausage

→ Ingredients


• Olive oil
• Chicken sausages
• Onion
• Garlic
• Bell pepper
• Diced tomatoes
• Chicken broth
• Italian seasoning
• Salt
• Pepper
• Spinach leaves
• Parmesan cheese (optional)

→ Instructions


1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add sliced chicken sausages to the pot and cook until browned.
3. Stir in diced onions and minced garlic, cooking until softened.
4. Add diced bell peppers to the pot and cook for a few minutes until slightly tender.
5. Pour in diced tomatoes and chicken broth, then season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10-15 minutes.
7. Once the flavors have melded, stir in spinach leaves until wilted.
8. Adjust seasoning if needed.
9. Serve hot, optionally garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
