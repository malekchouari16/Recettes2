CWalnut-Rosemary Crusted Salmon

→ Ingredients


• Salmon fillets
• Walnuts
• Fresh rosemary
• Panko breadcrumbs
• Olive oil
• Dijon mustard
• Honey
• Salt
• Pepper
• Lemon wedges (optional, for serving)

→ Instructions


1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a food processor, pulse walnuts and fresh rosemary until finely chopped.
3. In a bowl, combine chopped walnuts, rosemary, and panko breadcrumbs.
4. In a small bowl, mix Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.
5. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
6. Brush the top of each salmon fillet with the mustard and honey mixture.
7. Press the walnut-rosemary mixture onto the mustard-coated salmon, ensuring an even crust.
8. Drizzle a little olive oil over the crusted salmon fillets.
9. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the crust is golden brown.
10. Serve hot, optionally with lemon wedges for squeezing over the top.
