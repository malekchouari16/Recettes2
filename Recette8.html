Creamy Mustard Chicken Thighs

→ Ingredients


• Chicken thighs
• Olive oil
• Salt
• Pepper
• Onion
• Garlic
• Dijon mustard
• Whole grain mustard
• Chicken broth
• Heavy cream
• Fresh thyme
• Parsley (optional, for garnish)

→ Instructions


1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add chicken thighs to the skillet, skin-side down, and cook until browned and crispy.
4. Flip the chicken thighs and cook the other side until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside.
5. In the same skillet, add diced onion and cook until softened.
6. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
7. Stir in Dijon mustard and whole grain mustard, mixing well.
8. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
9. Stir in heavy cream and fresh thyme, then return the chicken thighs to the skillet.
10. Simmer until the chicken thighs are cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes.
11. Adjust seasoning if needed.
12. Serve hot, optionally garnished with chopped parsley.
