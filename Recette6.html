Open-Face Goat Cheese Sandwich

→ Ingredients


• Goat cheese
• Bread (sliced)
• Tomato
• Fresh basil leaves
• Balsamic glaze
• Olive oil
• Salt
• Pepper

→ Instructions


1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Place the slices of bread on a baking sheet.
3. Spread goat cheese evenly over each slice of bread.
4. Slice the tomato thinly and arrange the slices over the goat cheese.
5. Drizzle olive oil over the tomato slices.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the edges of the bread are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
10. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the top.
11. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Open-Face Goat Cheese Sandwich!
