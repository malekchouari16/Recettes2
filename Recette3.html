Green Goddess Grain Bowl

→ Ingredients


• Cooked grains
• Mixed greens
• Avocado
• Cucumber
• Cherry tomatoes
• Broccoli
• Edamame beans
• Radishes
• Green onions
• Hemp seeds or pumpkin seeds
• Green Goddess dressing

→ Instructions


1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, sliced avocado, cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, steamed or roasted broccoli, cooked edamame beans, sliced radishes, and chopped green onions.
2. Once the grains are cooked, divide them among serving bowls.
3. Arrange the mixed greens and vegetable mixture on top of the grains in each bowl.
4. Drizzle the Green Goddess dressing over the bowls.
5. Optional: Sprinkle with hemp seeds or pumpkin seeds for added texture.
6. Serve immediately and enjoy your Green Goddess Grain Bowl!
