Egg Drop Soup & Noodles

→ Ingredients


• Eggs
• Chicken or vegetable broth
• Instant noodles
• Green onions (scallions)
• Soy sauce
• Sesame oil
• Cornstarch
• Salt
• Pepper
• Optional: vegetables such as carrots, mushrooms, or bok choy

→ Instructions


1. Bring chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer in a pot.
2. Cook instant noodles according to package instructions, then set aside.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
4. Slowly pour the beaten eggs into the simmering broth, stirring gently to create egg ribbons.
5. Add sliced green onions, soy sauce, and sesame oil to the soup.
6. In a separate bowl, mix cornstarch with a little water to form a slurry, then stir it into the soup to thicken.
7. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
8. Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls.
9. Ladle the hot egg drop soup over the noodles.
10. Serve immediately, optionally with additional toppings like vegetables.
