Salmond Sushi Grain

→ Ingredients


• Salmon
• Sushi rice
• Avocado
• Cucumber
• Carrot
• Edamame
• Nori (seaweed)
• Sesame seeds
• Soy sauce
• Rice vinegar
•Sugar
• Salt

→ Instructions


1.Cook sushi rice according to package instructions.
2.Season the cooked rice with a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
3.Slice the salmon into thin strips.
4.Dice the avocado and cucumber.
5.Julienne the carrot.
6.Steam or boil the edamame until tender.
7.Toast sesame seeds in a dry pan until golden brown.
8.Arrange the seasoned rice in bowls.
9.Top the rice with salmon, avocado, cucumber, carrot, and edamame.
10.Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds.
11.Serve with soy sauce on the side.
12.Optionally, garnish with nori strips.
