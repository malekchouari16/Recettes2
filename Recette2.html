Beet & Shrimp Winter Salad

→ Ingredients


• Salmon fillets
• Mixed salad greens
• Cucumber
• Cherry tomatoes
• Red onion
• Kalamata olives
• Feta cheese
• Greek dressing
• Bell peppers
• Red or green cabbage
• Avocado
• Capers
• Fresh herbs

→ Instructions


1. Cook the salmon fillets (grill, bake, or pan-sear) until done.
2. In a large bowl, combine mixed salad greens, sliced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onion, pitted Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.
3. Place the cooked salmon fillets on top of the salad.
4. Drizzle the salad with Greek dressing.
5. Gently toss everything together to combine.
6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
