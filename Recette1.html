Greek Salmon Salad

→ Ingredients


• Salmon Fillet
• Romaine Lettuce
• Cherry Tomatoes
• Cucumber
• Red Onion
• Kalamata Olives
• Feta Cheese
• Bell Peppers (optional)
• Avocado (optional)
• Capers (optional)
• Olive Oil
• Lemon Juice
• Dijon Mustard (Optional)
• Garlic
• Oregano
• Salt and Pepper

→ Instructions


1. Prepare the Salmon: Season the salmon fillet with salt, pepper, and a bit of olive oil. Grill, bake, or pan-sear until cooked through. Let it cool slightly and then flake into large pieces.
2. Assemble the Salad: In a large salad bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, kalamata olives, and any optional vegetables like bell peppers or avocado.
3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl or jar, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, dijon mustard (if using), minced garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.
4. Dress the Salad: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
5. Add the Salmon and Feta: Gently place the salmon pieces on top of the salad and sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese.
6. Serve: Serve immediately and enjoy!
